Privacy Policy – AI Text Checker

Effective Date: November 2025

This Chrome Extension processes text locally in your browser to check for double spaces, space-hyphen-space (“ - ”), and occurrences of “ß” (Sharp S). All analysis and corrections happen entirely on your device.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, personal data, or browsing content. No information is sent to any external server.

Permissions Used

  • storage: Saves theme preference (dark/light mode) locally.
  • activeTab and scripting: Only used when you explicitly click “Scan & highlight” to analyze the current page’s visible text.

Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. No analytics or advertising services are used.
